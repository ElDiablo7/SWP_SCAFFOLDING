Scaffolding Safety Guide

Our commitment to safety is absolute. Discover the rigorous standards and compliance protocols SWP Scaffolding follows on every project.

UK Safety Standards

Working at height is inherently dangerous, which is why the UK has some of the strictest scaffolding safety regulations in the world. At SWP Scaffolding, we don't just meet these requirements—we exceed them.

TG20:21 Compliance

TG20:21 is the definitive guidance on safe erecting, altering, and dismantling of scaffolding. Every standard scaffold we build is erected in strict accordance with TG20:21 operational guides, complete with compliance sheets. Custom designs are engineered by qualified professionals.

SG4:22 Fall Prevention

We strictly adhere to NASC's SG4:22 guidance for preventing falls in scaffolding operations. Our scaffolders are equipped with advanced fall arrest harnesses and utilize safe working zones at all times during erection and striking.

Our Qualifications:

  • ✅ **CISRS Trained**: All our operatives hold valid Construction Industry Scaffolders Record Scheme cards.
  • ✅ **CITB Health & Safety**: Regular training and testing for all staff.
  • ✅ **First Aid**: Site supervisors are trained in emergency first aid.
  • ✅ **Advanced Scaffolders**: Available for complex, high-risk structures.

Mandatory Inspections:

By law, scaffolding must be inspected:

  • 🔍 Before first use
  • 🔍 Every 7 days whilst in use
  • 🔍 Following adverse weather conditions (high winds, heavy snow)
  • 🔍 After any substantial alteration
