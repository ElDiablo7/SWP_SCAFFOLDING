Case Studies

Detailed overviews of how SWP Scaffolding has delivered safe, compliant, and efficient access solutions for challenging projects.

COMMERCIAL SECTOR

Full Retail Wrap - Croydon High Street

A complex project requiring full perimeter scaffolding on a busy high street. The job involved detailed pavement licenses, out-of-hours erection to minimize public disruption, and heavy-duty loading bays for stone masonry work.

Challenge: Restricted public access
Solution: Suspended gantries and foam padding
DOMESTIC SECTOR

Emergency Temporary Roof - Sutton

Following severe storm damage, a residential property required immediate protection. We deployed a Keder temporary roof system within 48 hours, fully encapsulating the structure and preventing thousands of pounds in water damage.

Challenge: Urgent weather exposure
Solution: Rapid deployment HAKI truss system
INDUSTRIAL SECTOR

Plant Maintenance Access - Mitcham

Internal access was required for scheduled pipework maintenance inside a manufacturing plant. The scaffold had to be built around delicate, operational machinery without halting production.

Challenge: Live operational environment
Solution: Cantilevered drops and strict RAMS
